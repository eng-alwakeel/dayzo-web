About the Event

Thanksgiving is a national holiday in the United States, celebrated on the fourth Thursday of November.

History and Context

It originated as a harvest festival. The event that Americans commonly call the 'First Thanksgiving' was celebrated by the Pilgrims after their first harvest in the New World in October 1621.

Why People Search

It is one of the busiest travel times of the year, and families use the countdown to coordinate dinners and pre-holiday shopping (Black Friday).
