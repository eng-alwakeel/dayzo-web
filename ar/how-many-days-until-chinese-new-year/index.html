Chinese New Year, also known as the Lunar New Year or Spring Festival, is the festival that celebrates the beginning of a new year on the traditional lunisolar Chinese calendar.

It is one of the most important holidays in China and among Chinese communities worldwide, with traditions like red envelopes and dragon dances.
