About the Event

Earth Day is an annual event on April 22 to demonstrate support for environmental protection.

History and Context

First held on April 22, 1970, it now includes a wide range of events coordinated globally by Earthday.org including 1 billion people in more than 193 countries.

Why People Search

Schools, organizations, and individuals use the countdown to coordinate environmental awareness campaigns and local cleanup events.
