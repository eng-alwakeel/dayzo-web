About the Event

Independence Day, also known as the Fourth of July, is a federal holiday in the United States commemorating the Declaration of Independence of the United States, on July 4, 1776.

History and Context

The Continental Congress declared that the thirteen American colonies were no longer subject to the monarch of Britain and were now united, free, and independent states.

Why People Search

Americans search for this countdown to plan for parades, family reunions, and festive fireworks displays across the country.
