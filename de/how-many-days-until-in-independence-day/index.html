About the Event

Independence Day is celebrated annually on 15 August as a public holiday in India commemorating the nation's independence from the United Kingdom.

History and Context

On 15 August 1947, India became independent, and the first Prime Minister, Jawaharlal Nehru, raised the Indian national flag above the Lahori Gate of the Red Fort in Delhi.
