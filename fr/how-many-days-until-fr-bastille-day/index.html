About the Event

Bastille Day is the common name given in English-speaking countries to the national day of France, celebrated on 14 July each year.

History and Context

It commemorates the Storming of the Bastille on 14 July 1789, a turning point of the French Revolution.

Why People Search

People in France and fans of French culture track this countdown for the famous military parade on the Champs-Élysées and evening fireworks.
